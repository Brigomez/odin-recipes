Crispy Buttermilk Fried Chicken

You must try this recipe for buttermilk fried chicken! With a flavorful crispy coating, it has a juicy and succulent center, enhanced by the correct balance of herbs and spices. It is marinated in tart buttermilk and then coated in a flour mixture.

Simple to prepare: A clear, step-by-step recipe with all the advice you need to consistently produce juicy, soft, and perfectly crispy chicken pieces!

Easy buttermilk marinade: Using a buttermilk marinade is essential for the tastiest, crispy fried chicken. It adds a ton of flavor and tenderizes the chicken.

Packed with flavor: Each piece is pan-fried to a crispy, golden perfection and the crunchy batter is infused with a not-so-secret blend of herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Egg
  • Plain flour
  • Cornflour
  • Oil

Herbs and spices

  • Dried oregano
  • Sweet paprika
  • Onion powder
  • Celery salt
  • Dried thyme
  • Black pepper
  • Chili powder
  • Mustard powder
  • Dried basil
  • White pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Ground sage
  • Salt
  • Ground ginger

Step 1: Buttermilk brine

  1. Combine the beaten egg and buttermilk.
  2. Put the chicken pieces, salt, pepper, and paprika in a big bowl.
  3. Once the chicken is thoroughly coated, pour the buttermilk and egg mixture over the top and stir.
  4. Place the chicken in the refrigerator, cover it, and let it soak up the buttermilk brine for the entire night.

Step 2: Coat the chicken

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, cornflour, spices, and herbs.
  2. One by one, coat each chicken piece in the dry flour mixture. Before coating, gently shake off any excess buttermilk from the chicken.

Step 3: Fry the chicken

  1. To avoid packing the pan too full, fry the chicken pieces three to four at a time in the heated oil. Cook the chicken until it is cooked through in the center and has a golden brown color on all sides.
  2. While you finish cooking the other batches, place the finished chicken pieces on a wire rack resting over an oven tray and keep warm in the oven.
